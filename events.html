Nasscom Technology & Leadership Forum 2024

The experience at NTLF 2024 in Mumbai was nothing short of transformative. Day 1 buzzed with energy as industry leaders and professionals converged to shape the future of technology. Special thanks to organizers Sachin Mhaske and Hena Kausar for their visionary support. NTLF 2024 showcased the power of collaboration and collective knowledge, inspiring optimism for the future of technology.
